The Details

Location: Hurricane Lake Baptist Church
2516 Springhill Rd, Bryant, AR 72022

Date & Time: November 16, 2024 @ 2:00 PM

Dress Code: We invite our guests to embrace a dressy casual vibe for our wedding celebration. For women, this can include stylish dresses, nice blouses paired with tailored pants or skirts, and dressy jumpsuits. For men, we suggest collared shirts with slacks or chinos, and a blazer is always a great addition. Please avoid overly casual items like flip-flops or shorts, as we want everyone to feel festive and polished!
